About the Candidate

A Voice for Every Student

Bervel Brynx is a passionate and driven Economics student at the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST), with an unwavering commitment to uplifting the student community through bold, student-centered leadership.

With years of active engagement in student governance, academic committees, and community development programs, Bervel has earned a reputation as a transparent, dedicated, and innovative leader who listens—and acts.

His vision for the Economics Students Association is one of inclusivity, opportunity, and excellence—where every student has access to the resources, networks, and support systems needed to thrive academically and professionally.

Bervel believes that a strong ESA starts with trust and ends with transformation. His campaign slogan, "Together We Can," is not just a phrase—it is a promise.

Our 5-Point Agenda

A clear, actionable plan built for every economics student at KNUST.

📚
Academic Excellence Support

Establish peer tutoring networks, subsidized textbooks, and study resource hubs to ensure every student has access to the tools they need for academic success.

01
💼
Internship & Career Opportunities

Build strategic partnerships with leading firms, facilitate internship placements, and host career fairs that connect ESA members directly to industry professionals.

02
❤️
Student Welfare Improvement

Create a robust student welfare fund, improve mental health support, and advocate for better campus facilities and conditions for all economics students.

03
💡
Digital Innovation

Launch a dedicated ESA digital platform for resources, announcements, and community engagement — bringing governance and student services into the 21st century.

04
🏛️
Transparent Leadership

Implement open-book governance with regular town halls, published financial reports, and a direct feedback system so every student's voice shapes ESA decisions.
